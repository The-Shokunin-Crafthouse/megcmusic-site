# Intro email for Meg (copy-paste)

Subject: Adding shows to your site — right from your phone

Hey Meg!

You can now add shows to megcmusic.com without logging into WordPress. Just
email the details to **meghanclarisseshows@gmail.com** (save it as a contact —
maybe "Show Robot" 🤖) and it takes care of the rest.

Send one email per show, one detail per line, like this:

    Date: July 24
    Venue: Rock Rest Lodge
    City: Golden
    Time: 7pm-9pm
    Tickets: https://ticketlink... (only if there is one)

For an online show:

    Date: August 2
    Venue: Online
    Time: 7pm
    Stream: https://youtube.com/...

Within a few hours you'll get a reply showing exactly what will go on the
site. **Reply YES** and it goes live. If something's off, just reply with the
corrected line (like "Time: 8pm-10pm") and it'll fix it and check with you
again.

After you confirm, you'll get one more email with a small file attached for
Bandsintown: open artists.bandsintown.com, go to Events → Upload, drag the
file in, hit Publish. That keeps your BIT page in sync with about 10 seconds
of work.

Notes:
- It only listens to your email address (meghanclarisse@gmail.com), so
  nobody else can add shows.
- Nothing ever goes live without your YES.
- If a show changes after it's published, that edit still happens in
  WordPress for now — or just tell me.

— Levi
